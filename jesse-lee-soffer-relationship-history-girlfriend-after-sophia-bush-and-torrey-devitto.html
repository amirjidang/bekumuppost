Did Jesse Lee Soffer Date Anyone After His Complicated Relationships With Sophia Bush And Torrey DeV

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Highlights

  • Jesse Lee Soffer has had high-profile romances with castmates Sophia Bush and Torrey DeVitto.
  • Fans speculate if Soffer is dating his co-star Tracy Spiridakos.
  • Soffer rarely discusses his dating life publicly.

When Jesse Lee Soffer quit Chicago P.D. in 2023, it was tough news for fans who had been watching him play Jay Halstead in the Chicago franchise since 2013. Sadly, even though Chicago P.D. has been a huge hit, several cast members have said farewell and moved on. Even Soffer's co-star and former girlfriend Sophia Bush quit the show several years ago.

Viewers have been intrigued by the actor's love life since he has been romantically involved with two of his fellow castmates. Sophia Bush and Jesse Lee Soffer split up in 2015, and a few years later, he began dating Torrey DeVitto. According to Us Weekly, he and DeVitto went their separate ways in 2018 after an eight-month romance. But did Jesse Lee Soffer date anyone else in the past few years?

Who Did Jesse Lee Soffer Date After Sophia Bush And Torrey DeVitto?

While Jesse Lee Soffer is beloved for playing Jay Halstead on Chicago Fire, Chicago P.D., and Chicago Med, he also has some experience acting in a soap opera.

From 2004 to 2008 and again in 2010, he played Will Munson on As The World Turns. He also starred in the brief TV series The Mob Doctor and played Bobby Brady in the '90s movies The Brady Bunch Movie and A Very Brady Sequel. Many likely recognize him from his time portraying Bobby.

As Soffer's career has heated up, so has his love life. Since it has been a few years since his two high-profile romances ended, fans would love to know if the actor has dated anyone since 2018.

Based on how little information there is about Soffer's love life, the answer seems to be no. However, it's possible that he has been in a very private relationship this whole time and he hasn't said anything about it. It's also possible that he has had a few romances or even a handful of dates. Fans can only speculate as he hasn't confirmed any new relationships.

Some Chicago P.D. fans have wondered if Tracy Spiridakos and Jesse Lee Soffer are in a relationship. Since he has dated two castmates before, it makes sense that people would think this way.

In 2021, someone posted in the FauxMoi SubReddit and shared an e-mail that DeuxMoi had received: "Two actors from a cop show are dating for a while but apparently are keeping low key..."

One fan wrote, "Only cop show I can think of is Chicago PD, but I think all those people are in relationships except Jesse lee Soffer and maybe Tracey. They have terrible chemistry on the show though, so don’t think it’s them." A few fans disagreed with this statement and replied that they thought that sparks flew between the actors.

Another fan replied that they wondered if the cast members were seeing each other: "Chicago PD? Jesse Lee Soffer and Tracy Spiridakos? He dated Sophia Bush when she was on." A fan responded, "Tracy has been broken up with the guy you’re probably referring to since early 2019."

Even though Chicago P.D. fans thought that it was possible that the cast members dated each other, there has been no proof that this ever happened. The actors never confirmed a relationship or said that they even went on one date. While they always say nice things about one another, they seem to have a strictly platonic connection.

When the actor quit Chicago P.D., Tracy Spiridakos talked about Jesse Lee Soffer on social media. She sounded sad to see him go and it seemed clear that the two had a good professional relationship that was definitely friendly and warm.

According to TV Line, she shared in the caption of an Instagram post, “Jesse, what can I say, you’re the absolute best. Thank you for your kindness, your guidance and your friendship. You’re the greatest on-screen husband a girl could ask for. I’ll miss being on set with you every day. I can’t wait to see what the world has in store for you next. #upsteadforever.”

Fans also aren't likely to pick up any clues from the actor's Instagram account. He mostly shares news about his career. In the summer of 2023, he posted photos from his time on the picket line during the WGA/SAG-AFTRA strike. If he has been dating someone, he hasn't shared any pictures of them.

What Has Jesse Lee Soffer Said About His Dating Life?

Jesse Lee Soffer hasn't mentioned being in a relationship since he and Torrey DeVitto broke up. While he shared with Us Weekly that he was really content during his romance with DeVitto, he doesn't seem to talk about his love life very much.

However, the actor will discuss dating in more general terms. In 2023, Soffer was interviewed by Variety and participated in a game called "This or that?"

When asked to choose between sharing a "thirst trap" on social media or "slide into the DMs," he said, "I don't think I've ever slid in the DMs." He also said of texting or phoning someone, "Call. Texting is like... I hate it—I hate it." He also shared that he's a "total homebody" and his pals tease him because of it. It was endearing to hear this and further proof of why Chicago P.D. fans enjoy following along with Soffer's career.

In 2022 and 2023, Soffer's interviews focused on his choice to quit Chicago P.D. and what his career looks like now that he isn't filming the TV series anymore. He might not be that comfortable discussing his personal life, at least not right now. Perhaps if the actor does enter a new relationship, he will wait until things are official before discussing it publicly.
