Born on April 13, 1980, Kelli Giddish is an American television, stage, and film actress. She is well-known for playing the role of NYPD Detective Amanda Rollins in the NBC television legal crime drama series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

Before appearing in this drama series, she portrayed the role of Di Henry on the ABC soap opera All My Children. She also starred in the Fox crime drama series Past Life and the NBC crime drama series Chase.

Kelli Giddish was born in Cumming, Georgia to Charles and Nita Giddish. She has a brother named Eli. Her paternal grandfather encouraged her interest in acting and took her to the Fox Theatre in Atlanta.[3] Her maternal grandmother also supported her interests in the entertainment industry.

In 2005, Kelli made her acting debut on soap opera All My Children. In 2008, she appeared in the thriller film Death in Love (2008), which premiered at the Sundance Film Festival. In 2010, she appeared in the character of U.S. Marshal Annie Frost, in the drama series Chase.

In 2007, she made a guest appearance in Law & Order: Special Victims Unit in the episode “Outsider” as rape victim Kara Bawson. Later in 2011, she joined the cast of Law & Order: Special Victims Unit for its thirteenth season.

She was featured in the music video of his co-star Ice-T’s band for their newest album Bloodlust, which also features Soulfly lead singer and former Sepultura founder Max Cavalera.

On June 20, 2015, she was married to Lawrence Faulborn. She has two sons named Ludo and Charlie. Both of her pregnancies occurred while filming Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.
