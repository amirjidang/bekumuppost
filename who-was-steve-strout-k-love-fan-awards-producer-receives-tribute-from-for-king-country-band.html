Who Was Steve Strout? K-Love Fan Awards Producer Receives Tribute From For King &amp; Country Band

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Steve Strout’s obituary is shared on Facebook as most of his close ones have talked about him and his achievements. 

Strout was known for producing K-LOVE Fan Awards and a former national representative at Sony Music.

Before this, he worked at Christia Radio as well as at EMI CMG and Rocketown.

Prior to his career in the record industry, he programmed WHMX or Bangor.

Steve has come to attention after his death news surfaced on the internet. Sadly he had left the world leaving his dear and loved ones behind.

Steve Strout Obituary- What Is His Death Cause?

Following Steve Strout’s long battle with cancer, he succumbed to it at last and passed away.

Given his passing, several obituaries have come forward with condolences and prayers.

More on his illness, it first started with back ache. Steve had been having back pain for several weeks, and it slowly turned into muscle pain. It also so happened that he couldn’t keep the food down, which led him to be taken to the hospital by his wife AJ soon after the 2021 fan Awards.

Steve Strout went through several negative COVID tests, and lastly, blood test results determined that he had kidney failure.

He was admitted to the hospital where a CT scan of his abdomen revealed lesions on his pelvic bone. After being checked by a kidney doctor and oncologist, he underwent a renal biopsy, MRIs and CT scans, and a bone marrow biopsy.

Later, the reports confirmed that he had Multiple Myeloma cancer. That’s not all; he even had multiple lesions on his spine and a compression fracture on one of his vertebrae.

The medical personnel suggested him to undergo a stem cell transplant, an expensive procedure, to get it removed from his body.

Who Was Steve Strout? Wikipedia Bio Of The K-Love Fan Awards Producer

Steve Strout was an executive producer of the K-Love Fan Awards.

Originally from Bangor, Maine, he had been living with his family in Inlet Beach, Florida. Strout was born on September 12, 1972 but died untimely at the age of 49.

He completed his studies at Liberty University. He was a loving husband to his wife AJ Keatts Strout, whom he married on November 17, 2008, and father to three young children. Via his Facebook, he had credited his wife for being with him in sickness in unimaginable ways and for extensive dialysis training to be his caregiver.

Steve’s familiar ones and wellwishers have even organized fundraising and donations at Go Fund Me to help him and his family financially. And, the campaign has raised $75,185 USD raised of $150,000 goal till now.

Steve Strout Tribute By Joel From “For King And Country” Band

Steve Strout was tributed by Joel from For King And Country Band by in November 2021 through a short video on Facebook.

Joel had regarded Steve as one of his dearest friends and an unsung hero for him in the video. He even asked for help from the people and social media users and made people aware of the producer’s health condition.
