Guns, explosions, fighting and mortal danger? Sounds like the ultimate action film to watch! The Expendables is just that. The 2010 film was an accumulation of some of Hollywood's biggest action stars that went on to be a film franchise. It was directed by Sylvester Stallone, who also starred as the main lead.

The cast included some serious A-list action stars like Jason Statham, Jet Li and Terry Crews. They play an elite group of mercenaries tasked with taking down a Latin American dictator who is just a pawn in an elaborate game by an ex-CIA agent. With the film's main cast made up of the most well-known action stars, they have to have a sizable net worth to their names.

David Zayas ($4 Million)

David Zayas played a supporting role in the film as General Garza, the Latin dictator Barney (Stalone) and his crew are contracted to take down. Zayas has made his net worth as an actor in a variety of television and film appearances.

He is often recognized for playing Angel Batista in the 2006 show Dexter. Prior to Dexter, he had a role as Enrique Morale in OZ (2000-03). On Gotham, he played Sal Maroni for eight episodes in 2014 until 2015. In the 2014 musical film Annie, he played the bodega owner, Lou.

Giselle Itié ($5 Million)

The role of Sandra was played by actor Giselle Itié. Sandra was the informant for Barney and Christmas (Statham) when they arrived in Vilena. She is discovered to be General Garza's daughter. Barney becomes fond of her. Giselle Itié is a Mexican-Brazilian actor, who has made her net worth appearing in Brazilian films and television.

Itié has been in the shows Kubanacan (2003), Pé na Jaca (2006), O Profeta (2007), Bela, a Feia (2009) and Belaventura (2017). She has had roles in the 2012 adventure drama film, On the Road and Os Dez Mandamentos: O Filme (2016).

Eric Roberts ($8 Million)

In The Expendables, the ruthless ex-CIA agent who is using General Garza as a pawn for his grander scheme, James Munroe, was played by actor Eric Roberts. Roberts has been an actor for over 40 years with an extensive list of credits. In the late '70s and early '80s, he gained recognition for his roles in King Of Gypsies (1978) and Raggedy Man (1981).

He has since accumulated more roles in films. He played Dyson Keller in Wolves of Wallstreet (2002) and had roles in The Dark Knight (2008), Inherent Vice (2014), The Condemned 2 (2015) and Maximum Impact (2017). Roberts was the main role in the television series Less Than Perfect (2002-05) and played Vance Abrams in The Young and Restless (2012-11).

Mickey Rourke ($15 Million)

Mickey Rourke played the role of Tool. He was a supporting role seen in only two or three scenes in the film. Tool is a tattoo artist and owner of his own shop. He is also a friend of Barney and a recruitment liaison for the Expendables. He's also good with a knife. Rourke has made his net worth as an actor, screenwriter and former boxer.

Rourke retired from boxing in 1994 and dedicated himself to acting. Prior to leaving boxing, Rourke already appeared in various films including Barfly (1987), Angel Heart (1987) and Harley Davidson and the Marlboro Man (1991). In 2005, he returned to mainstream Hollywood in Sin City and starred in The Wrestler (2008). In Iron Man 2 (2010), he played Ivan Vanko/Whiplash.

Dolph Lundgren ($15 Million)

Dolph Lundgren played Gunner Jensen in The Expendables. Jensen can be referred to as the group's loose cannon. He battles with substance abuse and following Barney's orders. He is also an educated chemical engineer. Lundgren reprised his role in the following film sequels. Lundgren is a Swedish actor who had a huge breakthrough role in Rocky IV as the antagonist Ivan Drago.

Lundgren also has a degree in chemical engineering and is a trained martial artist. His films include Red Scoprian (1988), The Punisher (1989) and The Shooter (1995). He also directed the films Missionary Man (2007) and Command Performance (2009). He reprised his role as Ivan in Creed II (2018) and had a role in Aquaman (2018).

Randy Couture ($17 Million)

Actor Randy Couture, like many of his castmates, could actually be an Expendable in real life. Couture played Toll Road in The Expendables films. Couture is an actor, former army sergeant, professional mixed martial artist, and was even a UFC champion. He has made most of his net worth from acting and professional fighting

Couture is a three-time UFC Heavyweight champion, two-time Light heavyweight champion and interim Light heavyweight champion. He was the first of seven fighters to hold two championship belts in different weight divisions. He has had small roles in the show Hawaii Five-O (2015-17) and appeared as himself in Hell's Kitchen (2016) and Impractical Jokers (2018). He was in the Netflix film The Hard Way (2019).

Terry Crews ($20 Million)

Terry Crews played Expendable crew member Hale Ceasar, the more comical and muscular of the group. He also has a deep love for knives, big ones. Ceasar is a Heavy Weapons Specialist for the team. Crews has made his net worth as an actor, comedian, artist and former professional football player.

Crews played Julius Rock, from 2005 until 2008, in the sitcom Everybody Hates Chris by actor/comedian Chris Rock. He also played the role of Nick Kingston-Persons in the show Are We There Yet? (2010-12). Crews became the host of the game show Who Wants to be a Millionaire in 2014 until 2015. He then moved on to host America's Got Talent in 2019. On Brooklyn Nine-Nine (2013) he played the main role as Terry Jeffords. Crews is also known for his role in White Chicks (2004).

Stone Cold Steve Austin ($45 Million)

You really can't refer to Steve Austin by just his legal name. Autin played henchman Dan Paine in The Expendables. Stone Cold really needs to introduction seeing as wrestling and non-wrestling fans know who he is. Austin was one of the biggest WWE wrestlers in his time and is still considered one of the greats.

Everyone recalls Austin's iconic gimmick and beer chugging in the ring. He became a poster boy for the Attitude Era as an antihero who defied authority, did what he pleased and went against the boss, Vince McMahon. He held a total of 19 championship belts. Now he is an actor, producer and television host. He has appeared in the film, The Longest Yard (2005) and Grown Up 2 (2013). He hosted Tough Enough in 2011 and has a show titled Straight Up Steve Austin (2019).

Jason Statham ($70 Million)

Jason Statham is a well-known action star in Hollywood. He played Lee Christmas in the films. Christmas is Barney's second in command and friend. He is skilled in hand-to-hand combat and close-quarters knife combat. Statham is known for portraying Frank Martin in The Transporter (2002-08) film trilogy.

He also played roles in the action films The Italian Job (2003), The Bank Job (2008), The Mechanic films (2011). Statham also played the role of Deckard Shaw in the Fast & Furious franchise films. He also played Jonas Taylor in The Meg (2018).

Jet Li ($200 Million)

Trained martial artist and actor, Jet Li played Yin Yang in The Expendables films. Yin Yang is the team's martial arts expert who is often teased for his stature. Jet Li is known as one of the top martial arts/action stars in Hollywood. Li has appeared in many Hong Kong martial arts films.

He had roles in the films Dragon Fight (1989), Once Upon a Time in China (1991), Black Mask (1996) and Hitman (1998). His U.S. films include Lethal Weapon 4 (1998), Cradle 2 the Grave (2003), Fearless (2006), and Fordbidden Kingdom (2008). Li also played Emperor Han in The Mummy: Tomb of the Dragon Emperor and played the Emperor of China in Mulan (2020).

Sylvester Stallone ($400 Million)

Sylvester Stallone plays the main role of Barney Ross. Barney is the leader and founder of the Expendables, a trained group of mercenaries for hire. He specializes in sharpshooting and is the fastest gunslinger. Stallone is a famous Hollywood actor, director, screenwriter and producer.

He made name for himself in his breakthrough role as Rocky Balboa in the Rocky (1979-2018) series films. Stallone also played the iconic role of John Rambo in the Rambo films (1982-2019). Stallone played John Spartan in Demolition Man (1993), Judge Dredd in Judge Dredd (1995), Bullet to the Head (2012) and had a cameo in Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017). He directed four Rocky films, Rambo and The Expendables. He also co-wrote the first three Rambo films and The Expendables films.
