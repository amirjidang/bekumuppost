Kylie Jenner Accused Of More Plastic Surgery After Recently Admitting To A Boob Job Following Nearly

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Kylie Jenner's recent Instagram photos have sparked speculation that she has had more work done to correct past procedures, despite recently admitting to having a breast augmentation.
  • Fans on Reddit theorize that Kylie may have dissolved her facial fillers for a more natural look, as her cheeks appear flatter and her lips thinner in recent photos.
  • Kylie has not addressed the speculation over her facial fillers, but her previous admission to getting lip fillers only to dissolve them suggests she may have made similar changes to her face.

Kylie Jenner is once again sparking speculation that she’s had more work done, but the timing couldn’t be poorer, as the reality star only recently confessed to having a breast augmentation, nearly a decade after doing it.

Last weekend, the Kylie Cosmetic founder shared a carousel of images to her Instagram, with a number of them showing Kylie dressed in a flowy shirt and baggy jeans. But while reality star has her face turned away from the camera in several of the photos, it didn’t stop some folks from speculation that Kylie has gotten work again, but this time to correct past procedures.

Kylie May Have Dissolved Her Face Filler Like She Did With Her Lips

Not only did some of the comments on Kylie’s Instagram carousel result in criticism, but the photo was subsequently discussed on a Reddit thread, where a leading theory suggested Kylie recently had her facial fillers dissolved for a more natural look.

"She got a lot of her filler dissolved it looks like," one person wrote, The U.S. Sun notes.

"Yeah, I think so, and she looks so much better for it!" a different comment added, while another speculated, "I think her cheek filler was dissolved at least partially,"

"Her cheeks look way flatter to me, I was thinking something was up," one person added, whereas a different comment read, "Who even is that? Somehow looking different every time.”

So far, Kylie hasn’t addressed the speculation over her facial fillers. But if she did get them dissolved, it would be in line with what she recently did to her lips.

Though Kylie is famous for her large pout, she denied the use of lip fillers for years. She eventually confessed to beginning in 2015, only to get them dissolved in 2018 during her pregnancy with daughter Stormi. However, she’s since been getting them filled, but to arguably a lesser degree than before.

Kylie Just Admitted To A Boob Bob, But Says She Regrets It

Kylie has been rumored to have had a lot done, from a Brazilian Butt Lift to a chin implant. While she’s previously been silent on what work she has or hasn’t had done, Kylie has become more vocal since becoming a mom.

Earlier this year, Kylie revealed in an episode of The Kardashians that she had a breast augmentation at the age of 19, only to regret it soon after when she fell pregnant earlier than expected. "You know, I got my breasts done before Stormi," she said during a scene with her close friend Stassie. "Within six months of having Stormi, not thinking that I would like, have a child when I was 20. Like, they were still healing."

“I wish I never got them done to begin with,” she continued.

Given Kylie’s recent comments about regretting cosmetic enhancement, perhaps the Internet is right about her dissolving her facial fillers.
