'Married To Medicine' Cast Members Ranked By Net Worth

August 2024 · 7 minute read

Highlights

  • Married to Medicine showcases successful women linked to medicine, with net worth ranging from $1 million to $8 million.
  • Dr. Contessa Metcalfe built a $3.5M fortune through her medical concierge service, showing her skills as both doctor and businesswoman.
  • Bravo star Mariah Huq, worth $4M, is not just a cast member but also a producer, showcasing her vision and ability to succeed.

When it comes to reality TV, Bravo is one of the biggest television networks right now. They are responsible for some of the greatest reality TV shows ever, like Real Housewives, Vanderpump Rules, and of course, Married to Medicine.

The latter follows the lives of a group of women who are all somehow linked to medicine. The ladies of Bravo's Married to Medicine are either doctors themselves, work in the business side of medicine, or are married to doctors.

Due to their incredible careers and the success of the show, these ladies have managed to accumulate good fortunes, but who is the richest of them all? Let's find out!

Updated March 2024: Since the article was initially published, Phaedra Parks and Lateasha Luncefordhave joined the 'Married to Medicine' cast, and the list has been updated accordingly.

12 Anila Sajja

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 8-10

While there isn't a lot of information out there about one of the newer cast members Anila Sajja's sources of income and current net worth, it is estimated that she currently has a fortune of about $1 million. Anila is a blogger and influence.

Her blog, Peplum N Bubblegum, was very successful, and she used it to share fashion and lifestyle advice. Her content was mostly directed at mothers but it doesn't seem as if the blog has been updated in years.

11 Lateasha Lunceford

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Married to Medicine Season 10

Lateasha Lunceford joined the ladies of Married to Medicine for its 10th season. Latesha is married to Dr. Gregory Lunceford (who was previously married to Quad Webb), and she has worked on plenty of philanthropic business projects.

Lateasha has worked as a Pharmacy Tech, and she holds a degree in Integrative Studies. However, her net worth of $1 million doesn't put her too high on the list.

10 Quad Webb

Net Worth: $1.5 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 1-10

Quad Webb, now divorced from Dr. Gregory Lunceford, is said to be worth about $1.5 million. While she is not a doctor herself, her link to medicine is not just through her ex-husband, who is a psychiatrist.

Quad owes most of her net worth to her work as a medical sales representative and her TV appearances. In 2021, Quad Webb purchased a brand new home, however it didn't take too much time before she was accused of needing a $100,000 loan in order to buy it.

9 Dr. Jackie Walters

Net Worth: $3 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 1-10

Dr. Jacqueline Walters is an incredibly successful OBGYN. She has worked with celebrities like Usher and Toni Braxton, and she's currently worth $3 million. Like most doctors, Jacqueline is very strict about her own habits and makes sure to follow a healthy lifestyle. That includes her diet and her exercise routine.

She's a partner at Comprehensive Women's OB/GYN, and she's the founder of the 50 Shades of Pink Foundation, an organization that helps women suffering from breast cancer.

8 Dr. Contessa Metcalfe

Net Worth: $3.5 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 5-10

Dr. Contessa Metcalfe is a flight surgeon and works in preventive medicine. She also owns a medical concierge service called Acute Face MD, which is the main source of income that helped her build her reported $3.5 million net worth.

Working on her service, she became not only a very skilled doctor but also a businesswoman, which helped her improve people's quality of life while at the same time increasing her fortune.

Through the years, she has expanded her practice and opened a wellness center called Chastain Integrative Medicine, which helps clients learn to take better care of themselves. The Married to Medicine star is also very friendly with RHONY newbie Eboni Williams. In 2023, Dr. Contessa filed for divorce from her hubby, Scott.

7 Dr. Simone Whitmore

Net Worth: $3.5 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 5-10

Dr. Simone Whitmore is an OBGYN, and she owns a medical practice that operates like clockwork. She's also a member of the American College of OBGYN and the American Board of OBGYN, and her dedication to her profession is undeniable.

With her practice and her work with Married to Medicine, she made a considerable fortune, amassing a net worth of about $3.5 million. She's been married to Cecil Whitmore for many years, with whom she has two sons. In 2018, the two announced their divorce, but they haven't gone through with it — and are currently still together.

6 Mariah Huq

Net Worth: $4 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 1-7

Mariah Huq is not only part of the cast of Married to Medicine but she's also one of its creators and producers. Given that, it's not difficult to guess how she made her $4 million net worth.

Mariah is married to Aydin Huq, a successful doctor who specializes in emergency medicine at Emory University. It was probably her relationship that inspired her to do the show, and Bravo loved it. Evidently, the woman has a vision and is very good at her job.

5 Toya Bush-Harris

Net Worth: $4 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 1-10

Toya Bush-Harris is worth about $4 million, and she made her impressive fortune through her career as a pharmaceutical representative. She's married to emergency room doctor Eugene Harris, and the couple share what can only be described as a fairy tale love.

They met when Eugene was completing his residency and have been together ever since. Toya is also a children's book author. She wrote SleepyHead Please Go To Bed, a book inspired by her kids and designed to help parents worldwide.

4 Dr. Heavenly Kimes

Net Worth: $4 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 1-10

Dr. Heavenly Kimes has a very impressive career. She started her professional life as a dentist but has since also become an author, a businesswoman, and thanks to the Bravo show, a reality TV celebrity.

She has her own private practice and has written and co-written several best-selling books about succeeding in the business of medicine. All of those accomplishments added to her rise as a TV personality, have earned her a $4 million net worth. She's currently married to Dr. Damon Kime, and the couple has three children together. Heavenly sparked concern when she didn't show up for the season 8 reunion, leaving many fans wondering what was going on.

3 Phaedra Parks

Net Worth: $6 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 3, 10

Phaedra Parks joined the cast of the Bravo hit show as a replacement for Dr. Contessa Metcalfe and Anila Sajja. While she isn't a doctor herself, she is dating one.

However, Married to Medicine isn't Parks' first reality television experience. Prior to joining the cast, she was best known for being a cast member on The Real Housewives of Atlanta from 2010 until 2017. Considering she recently joined Married to Medicine, her $6 million net worth is certainly very impressive.

2 Kari Wells

Net Worth: $8 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 1-9

Kari Wells is a model from England who had a very successful career before she met Orthopedic surgeon Duncan Wells, with whom she fell in love and is now married to. The couple now shares two children together.

Since meeting Duncan, Kari has decided to devote her career to the business side of medicine. She was a Business major in England, so it came naturally to her. Now, she's the director of her own medical realty company, which supports many charities, including Doctors Without Borders. She has a net worth of $8 million.

1 Lisa Nicole Cloud

Net Worth: $8 Million

  • Married to Medicine Seasons 2-4, 8

Lisa Nicole Cloud started her career as a pharmaceutical sales representative after graduating from university, but with hard work and her studies, she expanded her career as a businesswoman.

She is now the CEO of Elite Marketing Strategies, Lisa Nicole Cloud Enterprises, and the Lisa Nicole Collection, and has a net worth of $8 million. She also founded her own clothing line, focusing on women's business outfits. She's married to Dr. Darren Naugles, and the couple have two beautiful children.
