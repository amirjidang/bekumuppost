A Peek Inside Calvin Klein's $25 Million Modern Hollywood Hills Home

During the early years in fashion in the late 1960s, Calvin Klein first became a design leader in the Jewish community in the Bronx before he earned recognition during the New York Fashion Week. Before venturing into different areas, the brand first sold coats and dresses and is best known for its jeans and underwear line. Calvin Klein has amassed $700 million net worth through his beauty empire and loves to spend his fortune on real estate. Being a native New Yorker, Calvin Klein has maintained his primary residence in the Bronx. However, he owns properties in Los Angeles and Miami. His last purchase occurred in 2015 when he paid $25 million for a stunning Hollywood Hills property that boasts a large space and contemporary amenities.

A home that was a blank canvas for the designer, Calvin Klein has added his artistic skills and his fashion house’s furniture elements to make it a perfect home.

Prime Location, Expansive Estate

Purchased in 2015, the home bought by Calvin Klein was a brand new abode. According to the Wall Street Journal, it is located on Tanager Way in the Bird Streets area of Hollywood Hills. The home was developed and listed by Drew Fenton and Tyrone McKillen, who represent Hilton & Hyland. Spread across 9,300 sq. ft. of the area, the contemporary home was designed by Paul McClean and featured five bedrooms, seven bathrooms, and multiple recreational rooms for a luxurious experience.

A Contemporary Entrance

Calvin Klein believed that there was nothing as important as the entrance of his abode. When he designed the entrance along with McClean, it turned into a contemporary masterpiece. The entrance offers a direct view of the entire estate made from glass and hardwood floors. It features a bridge that is located over a reflecting pool and waterfall. It leads straight to the open floor Great Room, Formal Dining Room, and kitchen on the Main Floor.

An Elegant Master Suite

Along with an open living space, the best feature of the Hollywood Hills Home is the Master Suite. An open concept from every direction, it offers scenic views of Los Angeles. The biggest room in the home is the stunning Master Suite designed in shades of gray, brown, and black. The chaise lounge is located near the floor-to-ceiling glass wall, connecting to the adjoining private terrace. The expansive terrace features a similar style of furniture and a stunning stone fireplace, as noted by Business Insider.

Dining Area And Additional Amenities

Rustic home with warm colors and neutral tones, Calvin Klein ensured that every room showed elegant living. Like many big-budget homes, the spacious home features a private screening room for twelve people with multiple chairs, couches, and a big television screen. After the house opens onto the Great Room, the space has an open Dining Room with an air-conditioned wine cellar. The Office and Family Room are located on the Bottom Floor.

Luxury Outdoor Amenities

The Hollywood Hills enclave has retractable glass walls from the floor to the ceiling, converging the indoor spaces with the outdoors. These converge the rooms together to form a larger area. As mentioned by LA Curbed, the 13-foot ceilings offer privacy to the fashion designer yet allow him to sink in the view of Los Angeles. These ceilings open towards the grass field next to the outdoor kitchen and barbecue station. Along the lines of the sun loungers is a 65-foot infinity pool that looks upon the tall treetops and view of the city.

Ample Garage Space

The home offers stunning views from the terrace of the top floor and has an adjoining eight-car garage that parks some of Calvin Klein’s priced automobiles, as reported by Luxury Pictures. The garage has a wall stacked with thirty-two rectangular photo frames that display abstract and real photographs along with minimal art pieces. One of the most notable cars in his collection is a stunning Rosso Corsa Ferrari.

Stunning Views And Famous Neighbors

The home's exclusivity also brings its perks as it offers scenic views of the Pacific Ocean and Downtown Los Angeles from every direction. Living in one of the most exclusive and sought-after neighborhoods, Calvin Klein has some famous guests as his neighbors who require privacy and seclusion similar to the fashion designer. His celebrity neighbors include Leonardo DiCaprio, Tobey Maguire, Matthew Perry, Jodie Foster, Keanu Reeves, Nikki Hilton, and many more.

Moving into a contemporary home, Calvin Klein created the most exclusive, sleek, and modern Hollywood Hills property seen on Bird Streets Avenue. The $25 million home is well worth its price, offering stunning views and open space. It is the perfect home for the fashion designer to maintain his privacy or host exclusive Hollywood gatherings.
