Are Ana De Armas & Jacob Elordi Close In Real Life?

The two seem to have a strictly professional relationship. In an interview with E! News in 2020, Elordi referred to De Armas as a "titan" in the industry. "Probably my favorite experience of recent was making Deep Water, and to kind of step back and watch these like titans of film make a movie," he said. "I could just sort of linger around and play a small role and you know be involved in a movie that I would really watch and enjoy." He also praised the Bond girl's work ethic, saying that it's inspiring to watch.

"They are such wonderful performers and they've really earned their stripes," Elordi said of De Armas and Affleck who were dating at the time. "We'd sit and jive a little bit, but I mean probably just watching what they do and seeing how they do it is a lesson in itself." In an interview with Variety, Elordi defended the former couple from fans who'd been invading their privacy. "I feel like the Internet is dedicated to that," he said of the fan pages dedicated to updates on the couple. "I feel so bad for them. Let them walk their bloody dog."

He also said that working with the Justice League actor felt surreal. "Like a dream. It was the most ridiculous thing ever," he said. "At first I just knew that Ben was in it. In my emails, it's Ben Affleck, do you know what I mean? He's one of the performers of my childhood, and just one of the all-time greats. We would do scenes from Good Will Hunting in acting school. I've seen every single movie that man's made."

Who Is Ana De Armas Dating Now?

Before dating Affleck, De Armas was married to Spanish actor Marc Clotet. In June 2021, five months after her split from Affleck, Page Six reported that De Armas had started dating Tinder VP, Paul Boukadakis. "Paul and Ana were introduced through friends," said the outlet's source. "He is based in Austin, but splits his time between Texas and Santa Monica. He has been spending a lot of time with Ana before she left the US to film her new movie." The two are still together as of March 2022.

The Knives Out actress reportedly had a "mutual and entirely amicable" split with Affleck and that "she broke it off" over the phone. "Their relationship was complicated," shared an insider. "Ana doesn't want to be Los Angeles based and Ben obviously has to since his kids live in Los Angeles." Despite ending things virtually, the source revealed that the breakup was "mutual" and "completely amicable." They added that the two were simply "in different points in their lives" but that "there is deep love and respect there."
