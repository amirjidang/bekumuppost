The legal drama series might have ended almost three years ago but the series has defied the expectations among the critics and stuck around long enough. With its likeable characters and the winning tone each episode, it is not surprising that the show reached nine seasons. Due to the show's popularity, USA Network even created a spin-off of the series, Pearson, which revolved around the character of Jessica Pearson. The longest running TV series of USA Network not only has a lot of brilliant actors but they are also loaded with cash. Take a look at the wealth of the cast members as of 2022.

Rick Hoffman

American actor Rick Hoffman played the role of fierce, unforgiving, associate-busting lawyer Louis Litt. Since the 90s, he has been portraying small roles on TV and movies. His first career break happened when he got a role in the film The $treet in 2000. Among his notable appearances include The Bernie Mac Show, Knight Rider, Philly, The Mentalist, CSI: Miami, and The Practice. His role as Louis Litt in the legal series has brought him enormous success and even made him the crowd favorite on the show. His estimated net worth as of 2022 is around $1 million.

Amanda Schull

American actress Amanda Schull played the role of a corporate lawyer loyal to name partner Louis Litt, Katrina Bennett. She was only a recurring role from seasons one to seven until she was made a permanent cast member from season eight onwards. Just like Scottie, Katrina made a mark among the series fans as Louis Litt's black swan who is always willing do whatever it takes to help Louis. Her estimated net worth as off 2022 is around $1 million.

Abigail Spencer

American actress Abigail Spencer played the role of a former senior partner at Pearson Specter and Harvey Specter's ex-girlfriend Dana Scott. Known as Scottie on the show, she was just a recurring role in the series but she did made a mark on the audience. She’s also had a recurring role in the famous series Grey’s Anatomy. Her current net worth as of 2022 is around $3 million.

Gabriel Macht

American actor and producer Gabriel Macht is also played a lead character on the show as New York City's best closer Harvey Specter. Macht earns his wealth from being an actor and as a producer. He started acting since he was in grade school and has started garnering positive reviews among the critics, and he’s had numerous acting nominations through the years. His current net worth as of 2022 is around $8 million.

Patrick J. Adams

Canadian actor and director Patrick J. Adams is playing the lead character for the series Suits as fraudulent lawyer Mike Ross. Some of his other notable roles include appearances in different projects such as Commander in Chief, Friday Night Lights, Heartland, Ghost Whisperer, FlashForward, Pretty Little Liars, Luck and Without a Trace. During his stint as Mike Ross in the series, he was nominated for different awards including Screen Actors Guild Award in 2012. Adams left the show after season seven because he thinks that the story of his character is done. His estimated net worth as of 2022 is around $10 million.

Meghan Markle

American humanitarian, philanthropist and former actress Meghan Markle is best known for her beautiful and talented paralegal turned lawyer Rachel Zane on Suits. Some of her other notable roles include films such as Remember Me, Get Him to the Greek, Horrible Bosses and A Lot Like Love. She left the series after season seven to join the royal family and get married to Prince Harry. Though she was married into the royalty, she and Prince Harry decided to split from the English Royal family to gain more freedom. Her estimated net worth as of 2022 is around $11 million.

Gina Torres

American actress Gina Torres played the role of former managing partner of Pearson Specter Litt and current aide to the mayor of Chicago Jessica Pearson. She was originally pursuing her singing career but decided to pursue acting instead. Among her notable performances include TV series such as Law & Order, NYPD Blue, Profiler, One Life to Live, and Xena: Warrior Princess. She is the richest cast of the TV series Suits. Her current net worth as of 2022 is around $15 million.
