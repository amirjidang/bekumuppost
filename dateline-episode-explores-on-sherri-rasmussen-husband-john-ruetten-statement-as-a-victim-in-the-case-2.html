Dateline Episode Explores On Sherri Rasmussen Husband John Ruetten Statement As A Victim In The Case

August 2024 · 2 minute read

John Ruetten was heartbroken after his wife Sherri Rasmussen’s 1986 murder, which brought emotional damage to many lives.

Sherri Rasmussen was beaten and shot to death on February 24, 1986. She was found dead in the apartment that she shared with her husband, John Ruetten.

The Rasmussen family believed till the end that John’s ex-girlfriend Stephanie Lazarus was the prime suspect in the tragic incident.

As the prosecutors proved, the LAPD officer Lazarus was indeed the culprit of the horrendous death of Sherri.

What Happened To Sherri Rasmussen Husband John Ruetten?

John Ruetten, Sherri Rasmussen’s husband, was present in the legal proceedings till the end and complied with the investigations.

John was truly heartbroken about his wife’s death but had never doubted Stephanie to be the murderer.

John had quit his job and left Los Angeles after Sherri’s death. Moreover, he had continued his physical relationship with Stephaine on his return.

However, after the conviction, his emotional distress was visibly felt in the court. He felt guilty that his wife died due to his actions of being involved with Stephaine.

John had apologized to the whole Rasmussen family for their loss in front of the court.

John Ruetten Victim Impact Statement Explained

John Ruetten described how his wife’s murder impacted his, and his family’s lives through his statement, later called a victim impact statement.

He tore up during the account of his sadness when he was talking about Sherri and sadly described his relationship with Stephanie.

CBS shared the emotional clip of John’s initiative in the cold case. Stephaine was not arrested until 2009, when the case was reopened with the aid of advanced technology.

Stephaine had bit Sherri’s arm, and the advanced DNA test later found her DNA remnants. Her jealousy and constant interference between John and Sherri led her to kill Sherri.

Moreover, the perpetrator went on to have a successful police career and even got close with John after the murder.

Where Are John Ruetten And His Family Now?

John Ruetten and his family’s current whereabouts are not available for public display at the moment.

He was so disturbed by his wife’s death that he left the city. Meanwhile, he had remarried and decided to stay away from the limelight.
