Who Is Gwen Keyes Fleming? Georgia DA Makes An Appearance On The Last Word

Gwen Keyes Fleming is the former district attorney of Georgia who recently appeared on the MSNBC news program The Last Word.

Amidst the ongoing investigations about former United States President Donald Trump’s effort to overturn the 2020 election results, the former DA appeared on the show expressing her take on the matter.

The Last Word is an MSNBC original news and political commentary show presented by Lawrence O’Donnell. Who Is Gwen Keyes Fleming?

Gwen is the first woman and the first African American to be appointed as District Attorney for the Stone Mountain Judicial Circuit in DeKalb County, Georgia.

Fleming has spent more than twenty years in the public policy and strategic planning sectors. She is currently the strategic partner on Environment Enforcement, Compliance, and Policy Matters at DLA Piper in Washington DC.

She is an excellent leader who has led several projects in Atlanta. She served as a DA for almost six years and during this time she administered more than $12 million budget and managed over 165 employees.

Georgia DA Gwen Fleming Wiki Bio

The two-time attorney is a notable alumnus of Douglass College at Rutgers University where she studied B.S. Finance. She finished her Doctor of Law from Emory University School of Law.

Gwen initially worked as a Solicitor General at the DeKalb County in 1999 before being appointed as the district attorney. She has also worked as a Chief of Staff in the US EPA after retiring for over four years.

Fleming was the principal legal advisor for U.S. Immigration & Customs Enforcement in 2015 where she served for two years. She is currently a partner at the national law firm Van Ness Feldman.

Who are Gwen Fleming Parents?

Keyes is a private person when it comes to sharing about parents and family.

Her mother Ursula Yvonne Keyes was a registered nurse and a member of the Presbyterian Church. Her father was Andrew J. Keyes Sr. who was a former Tuskegee Airman.

Both of her parents are dead now because of natural causes.

Gwen Fleming Husband and Family Details

The former attorney Keyes is married to her husband Randal Fleming.

Randal was born in Decatur, Georgia. He graduated from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville with a mechanical engineering degree. He attended the University of Georgia to complete his Masters in Information Technology.

Currently, Randal serves as the managing director of the property management company LiveTouch Works LLC based in Georgia.

Gwen is a proud mother of two sons named Corey and Kyle. Both of her boys are talented and she keeps on inspiring them.
