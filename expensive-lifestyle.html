Justin Bieber is a pop idol who rose from having his videos uploaded to YouTube by his mother to becoming one of the biggest selling music acts today. Aside from music, he also has a passion for vehicles. Here are some of the cars of Justin Bieber.

Justin Bieber with his Cadillac CTS-V

The Cadillac CTS-V is the high-end version of the standard Cadillac CTS. It boasts of a 6.2-liter V8 engine capable of producing 556 horsepower and 551 lb-ft of torque. It has been named as one of the 10 best cars by Car and Driver for the years 2011 and 2012. Bieber’s black CTS-V has been modified to look like the Batmobile. He has been in a couple of trouble with the vehicle. He once cut off a police cruiser while leading a motorcade with other artists like Sean Kingston. He was also caught making an unsafe left turn. Bieber got off both times with a warning.

Justin Bieber with his Campagna T-Rex 14R

 

The Campagna T-Rex is a Canadian-made motorcycle that has three wheels and two seats that can accommodate the driver and the passenger side by side. It has a 1.4-liter straight four engine. Its features include an adjustable seat back, foot pedal box and three-point retractable seat belts. Rob Dyrdek gifted A 2007 version of the vehicle to Bieber back in 2011. It had a purple color with Bieber’s initials embossed in the front, as well as the quote “Make Your Own Luck” painted into the side. It was recently sold through an eBay auction for $35,000, with the proceeds going to charity.

Justin Bieber with his Ferrari F430

The Ferrari F430 is an Italian sports car produced from 2004 to 2009. It was later on replaced by the Ferrari 458 Italia, another car that Bieber owns. The Ferrari F430 has a 4.3-liter V8 engine and a six-speed electrohydraulic manual F1 transmission. Ferrari and Maserati derive the engine from a shared design. It is one of the few vehicles in the United States that is exempted from the airbag requirement. Bieber’s Ferrari 430 sports a matte black color.

Justin Bieber with his Ferrari 458 Italia

The Ferrari 458 italia is a mid-engined car introduced in 2009 as a replacement for the Ferrari F430. It has a 4.5- liter V8 engine with seven-speed dual clutch transmission. It is a completely new design and includes technologies learned from Ferrari’s participation in Formula 1. The engine comes from a joint venture with Maserati while the transmission is shared with that of the Mercedes Benz SLS AMG. It has garnered Car of the Year honors from CAR Magazine, Auto Express, What Car, Daily Telegraph and Fifth Gear. Bieber’s Ferrari 458 Italia has a white-on-white concept designed by West Coast Customs.

It has been involved in a number of mishaps, however. A paparazzi was once killed by another motorist as he was trying to photograph the Ferrari after it got pulled over. He thought Bieber was on the wheel, but his friend Lil Twist was actually driving it. Lil Twist also once damaged the car when he bottomed it out as he was exiting the Four Seasons in Beverly Hills. It was also pulled over while another friend, Lil Za, was driving it because of its extremely dark tint. Bieber was also pulled over once for making an unsafe left turn and for having expired registration.

Justin Bieber with his Fisker Karma 

The Fisker Karma is a plug-in hybrid luxury sports sedan. It has a 2-liter turbocharged combustion engine and two 161 horsepower electric motors. It was launched in 2011 and has a total range of 230 miles or 370 kilometers. It costs more than $100,000. Bieber’s Fisker Karma has an all-chrome look. It was a gift from his manager Scooter Braun when he Bieber turned 18 years old. He was riding this car when he got pulled over for doing 100 miles per hour on a 65 mile per hour area in a Los Angeles freeway. Bieber claimed he was going fast because a group of paparazzi was chasing him.

Justin Bieber with his Lamborghini Aventador

 

The Lamborghini Aventador is a two-door sports car that replaced the company’s classic Murcielago as its new flagship model. It has a 6.5-liter V12 engine with a seven-speed semi-automatic transmission. It has a responsive engine and torque and a smooth power output. It has been adjudged by Car and Driver as the best Lamborghini ever made. Top Gear also voted it as the “Supercar of the Year” in 2011. The specialty shop called RDB LA was the one that modified Bieber’s white Aventador.

Justin Bieber with his Lamborghini Gallardo Spyder

The Lamborghini Gallardo is a super car that boasts of a 5.2-liter V10 engine and six-speed transmission. Bieber got this white vehicle in 2010 when Sean Diddy Combs presented it to him as a gift for his 16th birthday. Bieber did not really think Diddy was serious when the latter promised it to him months before his birthday, so he was pleasantly shocked when Diddy made good on his promise.

Justin Bieber with his Mercedes Benz Sprinter

The Mercedes Benz Sprinter is a commercial vehicle with a front engine, real wheel drive layout. It has a six-speed transmission and the top of the line model boasts of a 3.5-liter V6 engine. West Coast Customs customized Bieber’s matte black Sprinter for him.

Justin Bieber with his Porsche 997 Turbo

The Porsche 997 Turbo is a supercar that has a 3.8-liter H6 engine. It is actually an internal designation of the company for the Porsche 911 model. It is also known as the Porsche Carrera. It is considered the most successful 911 of all time. Bieber’s 997 Turbo is black with red wheel rims.

Justin Bieber with his Range Rover

 

The Range Rover is a four-wheel sports utility vehicle that boasts of a 5-liter V8 engine and eight-speed automatic transmission. The British-based A. Khan Design customized the vehicle for Bieber. It has a flat black color, customized bumpers and lowered suspension. Bieber also got a Range Rover from Usher in 2010 as a gift for his 16th birthday.

Justin Bieber with his Smart Car

Bieber also bought himself a Smart Car. He had it customized to a flat black color and had the name “Smart Car” changed to “Swag Car.”
