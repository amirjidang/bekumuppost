Barbara Wernick is one of those celebrities who came to the spotlight after being married to a celebrity.

Married Life

She is a private person when it comes to her personal life. Her husband is no different. 

Both of them have not revealed the wedding details like the wedding date and venue.

We are not sure how many children they have as they have never mentioned having children. 

Who Is Adam Sandler?

Adam Sandler is an actor who was discovered by the talent manager, Sandy Wernick. 

Adam is estimated to have a jaw-dropping net worth of $420 million. 

He earns an average salary of $20 million per movie. When Sandy discovered him, he was just 22 years old. 

Like every other client, Sandy encouraged him as well. 

When he became a well-known actor, he thought of paying off his talent manager’s debt. 

To do so, he made a movie titled Sandy Wexler that was based on his talent manager’s life in the ’90s in Los Angeles. 

The film was released in 2017. 

In the movie, Adam himself portrayed the character of Sandy, who ties the wedding knot with a fellow client, Courtney Clarke. 

The role of Courtney was played by Jennifer Hudson, who has a staggering net worth of $25 million. 

The biopic movie made Sandy even more popular. 

Other casts of the movie included Kevin James, Terry Crews, Shad Gaspard, and Rob Schneider, who have a net worth of $100 million, $25 million, $1 million, and $15 million respectively.

Both Sandy and Adam have worked together on many projects.
