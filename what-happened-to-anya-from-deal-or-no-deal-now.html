Anya Monzikova’s Deal Or No Deal Legacy

Every time a new contestant took to Deal or No Deal’s stage in hopes of winning $1 million, 26 models walked out on stage in unison, each of them clutching a briefcase.

As a result, that meant that every single woman who stepped out on that stage with a briefcase in tow was surrounded by 25 other gorgeous women. In that situation, most people would struggle to stand out no matter how beautiful they were.

After Deal or No Deal premiered on television in 2005, Anya Monzikova became one of the show’s models in 2006. Just like every other Deal or No Deal model, Monzikova constantly was surrounded by many gorgeous models and the camera typically only focused on her for seconds during each episode she was in. Despite all of that, there was something about Monzikova that allowed her to stand out.

Unfortunately for everyone involved with Deal or No Deal’s production and its fans, the show’s initial run came to an end in 2009. According to IMDb, since Anya Monzikova was a part of Deal or No Deal from 2006 until 2009, she appeared in 193 episodes of the hit game show which is a pretty impressive number, to say the least.

Where Is Anya Monzikova From Deal Or No Deal Now?

Throughout television history, there have been a lot of people who’ve failed to move on after starring in a hit show for years. After Anya Monzikova was a part of Deal or No Deal from 2006 until 2009, she easily could have found herself in a similar situation.

Fortunately, however, Monzikova began branching out her life and career while she worked on Deal or No Deal so she wasn’t left flat-footed.

In 2007, Anya Monzikova began to launch a second career as an actor when she appeared in TV shows like Medium and CSI: Miami. Once her Deal or No Deal tenure came to an end, Monzikova began to enjoy some traction in her acting career.

Able to land small roles in movies like Surrogates and Iron Man 2, Monzikova has also popped up in shows like Melissa & Joey, Bones, The Client List, Days of Our Lives, and Ray Donovan.

In recent years, Anya Monzikova has continued to work as an actor off and on, more often than not as a part of indie films. Some examples of Monzikova’s more recent acting projects include movies like Seeking Dolly Parton, Turned Out, How High 2, as well as Wine & Whimsy. While it is great that Monzikova continues to express herself through acting, her main focus seems to be elsewhere these days.

On Instagram, Anya Monzikova goes by @anyamonzikova and is quite active. As her social media posts make very clear, Monzikova has become a wife and mother. Married to Josh Fritts who works in the entertainment industry as a Director of Photography, the couple has welcomed their two children into the world.

On top of being a model, actor, wife, and model, Anya Monzikova also has been a blogger in recent years. As the person behind sippycupsandrose.com, Monzikova shares insights into the food she makes and the home renovations she completes on that website.
