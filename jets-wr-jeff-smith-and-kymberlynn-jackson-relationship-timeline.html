Jeff Smith And Kymberlynn Jackson Relationship Timeline

Jeff Smith and Kymberlynn Jackson began dating in 2020 right before the pandemic hit the world.

At the time, Kymberlynn was a college junior, and she made the wide receiver work hard to gain her attention. And Jeff didn’t mind that at all, as he would work around the schedule to visit her in California. 

Pandemic Love 

The couple enjoyed their first date right after Kym celebrated Valentine’s Day with her gal pals.

After covid hit and school were canceled, Kym decided to travel to Florida to spend time with her new love. Their budding romance grew stronger daily as they spent more time with each other. She also started to make trips to New York to cheer on her partner as he trained with the Jets. 

From leaving cute notes to enjoying picnics, the pair share a healthy relationship, a sight that can’t be seen much in an athlete’s life until they truly decide to focus on it. As they started to meet frequently, Jackson also decided to bring Grace, who has now become friends with Bella, Smith’s pet dog.

The relationship started to take a much more serious turn in 2021 as she introduced Jeff to her family, with the brunette even sharing photos of him meeting her dad on her Instagram handle.

Together the couple also has invested in properties and looks to grow their business furthermore in the future. 

Jeff And Kymberlynn Engagement 

Jeff and Kymberlynn got engaged on 2 January 2022 after the wide receiver went down on one knee to propose to Jackson.

He set up a big sign that read MARRY ME at Laguna Beach, California, and hired a violinist to make the occasion more romantic. 

The excited future wife of the wide receiver took to her Instagram to share the images from the romantic proposal he had organized for her. Jackson showed the beautiful diamond ring and shared a video from that day.

Both their families were present for the special occasion as Kym enjoyed a tender moment with her father as they cried and hugged each other. 

A New Addition To Their Family

The pair announced they would soon be parents three months after their engagement. Kym posted a gallery of images showcasing her growing baby bump.

The couple at the time was enjoying a trip to Cancun, Mexico, and shared several photos from the trip. Kym’s fans and friends rushed to offer their congratulations following the great news.

On June 26, 2022, the couple had a “gender reveal” ceremony with their family and friends as they learned that Kym was growing a baby boy in her womb. Then finally, on July 17, 2022, the couple became parents to Jayce Kamrynn Smith. 

The little bestie of Kym accompanies him from the malls to the stadium of the New York Jets as they watch Jeff take on the field. 

Wedding Bells Are On The Cards

Kym revealed that she and Jeff were planning their wedding ceremony and searching for a venue in October.

In her latest post, Kym was seen doing some late-night research with her fiance as their hunt for the perfect place for their special occasion continues. They have settled on the date and location. Though they have not publicly announced it to their fans, their wedding website shows they plan to be pronounced husband and wife on April 2, 2024.
