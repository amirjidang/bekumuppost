The Cast Of Amazon's 'Upload,' Ranked By Net Worth

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Upload is a sci-fi comedy/drama series that premiered exclusively on Amazon Prime Video on May 1, 2020. The series was quickly renewed for a second season, which is set to premiere on March 11, 2022.

The first season premiered ten episodes. However, it is unclear how many the second season will have. According to Prime, the description of the show is, "in the future people can upload their consciousness to a luxurious digital afterlife. When party boy Nathan gets uploaded to a virtual resort he meets the down-to-earth Nora who starts as his customer service “angel”, but becomes so much more as she helps him find friendship, love and purpose."

Starring Robbie Amell as the main character, it is free to watch as long as you have a Prime account. Before the new season premieres, let's take a look at the cast of Upload. We ranked them by lowest to the highest net worth.

8 Josh Banday - Unknown

Josh Banday plays Ivan, Nora's coworker, who will have a more permanent role in season 2. He has also had guest appearances on TV shows such as The Big Bang Theory, Mom and Adam Ruins Everything. Banday is currently starring in the series Pam & Tommy. Although he has some acting credits under his belt, his net worth is not known publicly.

7 Owen Daniels - Unknown

Owen Daniels plays A.I. Guy, an employee at Lake View. He is an actor and writer best known for his role in Space Force. He also made appearances in The Office, which was developed by his father, Greg Daniels, who also directs and created Upload. His net worth is unknown, however, as he has only starred in those two shows.

6 Zainab Johnson - $200,000 To $300,000

Zainab Johnson is a comedian and actress who plays Aleesha, Nora's co-worker, who is Luke's handler. Although her career started on Last Comic Standing, this is her first serious, scripted role. She also appeared in the HBO special All Def Comedy, and she hosts the Netflix series, 100 Humans. All of this puts her net worth around $200,000 to $300,000, although there is no clear answer.

5 Allegra Edwards - $700,000

Allegra Edwards plays Ingrid Kannerman, Nathan's girlfriend. Edwards has not acted in too much, but is a regular in Upload and the anthology series, Briarpatch. She is married to Clayton Travis Synder, who played Ethan Craft in the show, Lizzie McGuire. The 34-year-old has also appeared in episodes of Modern Family, Orange Is The New Black, New Girl and The Mindy Project. Her net worth is not confirmed, but most websites put her in the $700,000 range, in which she shares with her husband.

4 Andy Allo - $1 Million

Andy Allo plays Nora Anthony, a woman who is alive and is Nathan's handler in the afterlife. You might also know her from Pitch Perfect 3 and Chicago Fire. She also has a reputable music career as she was a singer and guitarist in Prince's band, The New Power Generation, in 2011. She has released five studio albums. Although her net worth is different from site to site, most of them put her at about $1 million.

3 Robbie Amell - $2 Million

Robbie Amell is the main character, Nathan Brown, a 27-year-old computer engineering grad and newly deceased computer programmer who is uploaded to the digital afterlife, Lakeview. Amell's net worth is estimated to be $2 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He has earned that net worth through his time as an actor and model. Amell is best known for starring in The Flash on the CW and has also starred in Cheaper By The Dozen 2, True Jackson, VP, The Duff and more.

2 Kevin Bigley - Around $3 Million

Kevin Bigley plays Luke, a former army corporal and a resident of Lakeview who befriends Nathan. His net worth is not confirmed, but most sites put him in between $1 to $5 million, with $3 million appearing the most. He, along with his wife, actress Kate Cobb, earned that money from acting. Bigley has starred in Sirens, The Dilemma, The Angry Birds Movie, Undone and more.

1 Chris Williams - $4 To $5 Million

Chris Williams plays Dave Antony, Nora's father. He is an actor, voice actor and comedian, who is the younger brother of actress Vanessa Williams. He is known for his roles in Dodgeball: A True Underdog Story, The Great Indoors and Curb Your Enthusiasm, among other roles. Although not confirmed many websites put his net worth between $4 and $5 million, which would make sense considering the amount of movies and shows he has starred in, along with the video games he lends his voice to.
