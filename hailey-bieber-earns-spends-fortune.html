Born in Tucson, Arizona, in 1996, Hailey Baldwin-Bieber earned fame as a model and actress before becoming even more famous as the spouse of the Canadian pop star Justin Bieber. Hailey was born in the family of the famous Baldwin brothers, where her father, Stephen Baldwin, was the youngest of the brothers. She comes from a family of mixed origins as her mother is a Brazilian with Portuguese and Italian descent while her father is of English, Irish, Scottish, French, and German origins.

Hailey grew up in an environment of cultural assimilation and a spectrum of talents in music, entertainment, and graphic designing. Hailey dreamed of being a ballet dancer when she was young, but she had to give up on that dream when she suffered a major foot injury. However, Hailey did not suffer from dejection because her dream was over. Instead, she got back on her feet and tried her luck in show business.

Today, Hailey Baldwin-Bieber has come a long way and has an impressive net worth of about $20 million. Here is how the 24-year-old celebrity earns and spends her million-dollar fortune.

Earns: Her Career In Modelling

Hailey Baldwin Bieber decided to try out modeling when her dream to become a classic ballet dancer was shattered due to a leg injury. Her first success in this endeavor came in the form of a contract with the international agency Ford Models.

She signed this contract back in 2014, and two years later, she landed another deal with IMG Models. Hailey has since made some fancy bucks with frequent appearances on the covers of reputed magazines like Vogue. In 2019, she became a sensation when she walked in Versace's pre-fall show as it was her first show as Mrs. Bieber.

Spends: Real estate

The Bieber couple is famous for spending generously on luxury estates and mansions ever since they tied the knot. Before which, Justin Bieber was fond of living in rented mansions and penthouses, as he preferred the traveling lifestyle. Hailey and Justin also decided not to live under the same roof before marriage.

After tying the knot, the couple purchased a luxurious home in Beverly Hills in 2018 for a grand $8.5 million. It was a well spread-out colonial home with five bedrooms, six bathrooms, and an infinity pool amongst a hoard of luxury amenities. However, after two years, they decided to sell it and buy a $25.8 million worth mansion in a gated community in Beverly Park. This 11,145 square feet mansion came with more bedrooms and bathrooms, which was definitely an upgrade from their previous purchase. Another property that the couple bought was a $5 million estate in Ontario, which Hailey refers to as Justin’s ‘most comfortable space.’

Earns: YouTube

Hailey has a channel on YouTube under the name ‘Hailey Rhode Bieber.’ The fact that Hailey commands even more popularity is proved by the number of subscribers she has on the channel.

She created this channel on 6th March 2021, and within a few months, she already gained about 1.17 million subscribers. The model also put up an Instagram post flaunting her YouTube Silver and Gold Creator Awards.

Spends: Luxury Cars

The car collection of Hailey Bieber is no match to Justin Bieber’s, but it is definitely the collection of a connoisseur. While Justin owns around 11 supercars, Hailey has approximately three. Her collection is, nonetheless, quite impressive.

The model owns a Rolls Royce Ghost worth about $300,000, and this silver beast is the definition of elegance. Hailey also owns a classy black Mercedes S Class that cost her a significant amount of about $120,000. In addition, another $100,000 Black Cadillac Escalade has been a part of her garage for a long time now.

Earns: Endorsement Deals

Modeling has been a lucrative career for Hailey as it has also paved the way for her to crack the best endorsement deals. Moreover, after her wedding with Justin Bieber, she has bagged more and more brand deals.

She has worked with the most prestigious labels like Guess, Ralph Lauren, and Tommy Hilfiger. She also launched a line of handbags called ‘#theHAILEYedited’ collection in collaboration with the fashion accessories brand The Daily Edited.

Spends: Travel And Partying

Justin Bieber has always been keen on traveling even before marriage, and life has been particularly significant for the couple since both love to travel. Hailey also has a history of traveling as her career in modeling demands her to embark on new journeys regularly.

The couple often finds time for romantic getaways and vacations. Since Justin and Hailey are relatively young, both are naturally party animals and end up spending some big bucks in their endeavors.

Earns: Hosting Shows And Performing In Music Videos

Hailey has also made a huge chunk of her fortune from the entertainment industry by hosting different shows. She hosted a part of the MTV Europe Music Awards in 2015.

She landed another hosting gig two years later for the TBS show called Drop The Mic together with Method Man. Hailey is also famous for starring in different music videos like Cody Simpson’s, On My Mind, Baptiste Giabiconi’s Love To Love You Baby, Dan + Shay and Justin Bieber’s 10,000 Hours, Ariana Grande and Justin Bieber’s Stuck With U, and DJ Khaled, Drake, and Justin Bieber’s Pop Star.

Spends: Fashion Accessories and Cosmetics

Given Hailey’s history in modeling, it comes as no surprise that she is picky when it comes to fashion. However, the interesting bit is that the Bieber couple splurged a fortune for designing the gown of Hailey for their wedding.

Hailey is also very conscious about her skin routine as she believes in maintaining her stunning looks for the camera. She uses expensive skincare products like the Furtuna Skin Due Alberi Biphase Moisturizing Oil that costs about $128.

Fans often get excited to know how Hailey and Justin live their life together. From their social presence and shots from the paparazzi, it can be said that the couple is living ‘the dream.’
