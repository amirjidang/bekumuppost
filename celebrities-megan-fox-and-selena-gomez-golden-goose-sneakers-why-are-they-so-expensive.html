They have all sorts of money and can really buy anything, yet they choose to pay hundreds of dollars for dirty, worn out shoes? Yep. Similar to the jeans we purchase, these shoes are not worn out, old, or even dirty. They are hand painted to look that way. What brand are we talking about? Golden Goose sneakers, of course. They are easy to spot because they have fake scuffs and dirt marks all over the upper, and they are usually on the feet of a celebrity. That's because they cost anywhere from $500 to $2,000 a pair.

Why Are Golden Goose Sneakers So Expensive?

Golden Goose sneakers are more expensive than the vast majority of Nikes, Guccis, and Pradas. Not quite as expensive as Louis Vuitton or Kylie Jenner's Balenciaga's but those are really extreme.

Golden Goose sneakers are handmade in Italy. Like most products that come from Italy, a lot of attention to detail goes into the product. Golden Goose sneakers are no different. Every pair of shoes that a customer buys has been through a long, hard process by a professional. Both the painting and the distressing of the shoes are also done by hand.

You can bet that these people are not making a simple minimum wage. It is a known fact that Italian shoemakers, and those who work for them, make a good living at this job. That wage needs to be paid somehow, so like all products, it's tacked onto the price.

Being handmade, the shoes are incredibly comfortable, and the leather is so soft. Pictures don't really do them justice; seeing them in person, you can really see a difference between them and your everyday casual sneaker. For example, Cardi B Reebok's vs Golden Goose, you would notice a big difference in the stitching and the leather.

The uniqueness of Golden Goose sneakers is possibly one of the main factors contributing to their high price. Each pair of shoes is unique. Even a pair of shoes is not exactly the same. Each one is as distinctive as a person's fingerprint thanks to the subtle variations in their distressing.

One pair of shoes may have more color in the distressing than the other, for instance. Another might have a pattern that is slightly different. The skid mark on one shoe may be shorter or completely absent on the other shoe. Golden Goose purposefully does this to show how unique they are.

What Are Golden Goose Sneakers Made Of?

Golden Goose's shoes are expensive in part because of the materials that are used to make them. Italian clothing is known for using the highest-quality leather. Golden Goose shoes do too. Calfskin leather is the main type of leather used to manufacture their shoes.

Due to the fact that it is made from calf hides, this leather is expensive. Italy holds its leather to incredibly high standards.

To maintain their calves' best health, farmers incur significant costs. The calf's skin and, consequently, its leather are better the healthier the animal is. Since calfskin leather costs a lot to produce, it has a high cost of sale.

What Are The Best Dupes For Golden Goose Sneakers?

There are plenty of dupes for Golden Goose sneakers, but for the most part, they're based on looks alone. The public has more often than not, never seen Golden Goose sneaks up close and personal, so it's likely they won't be able to tell the difference.

If you are looking to tell the difference, Golden Goose has a trademark on where the star is placed on their shoe. No other shoe can place the star in that spot on look-a-likes.
