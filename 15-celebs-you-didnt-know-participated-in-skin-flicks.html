Just missing the list is Joanie Laurer (aka Chyna) and Hulk Hogan - first, because they are wrestlers, and second, because their films (the former intentional, the latter not) were released after they were famous. Same goes for Jane Fonda - just because you have a lull in a successful career doesn’t make it right. Of course Kim Kardashian is worth a discussion here, but her fame is complicated and so is her amateur film. Fun fact: Did you know the kid who gets his tongue stuck in the movie A Christmas Story did an adult movie? The holidays will never be the same.

Come and take a look at what we found…we’ve got action heroes, respected Oscar winners and yeah, a few you just knew started acting with their pants off. Don’t judge, it could happen to anyone! Now let’s see who made our list of Hollywood stars that got their career kick-started working in the skin industry.

15. Matt LeBlanc

Joey from Friends wasn’t always so naive. Matt LeBlanc was making bank by the end of his run with Friends, but it wasn’t always that easy for him. LeBlanc starred in Red Shoe Diaries, a show from Playboy Entertainment that was shown late at night for lonely or bored insomniacs. Most episodes, including the ones with LeBlanc were very predictable and low on acting skill. Still, it was a stepping stone for many, including LeBlanc who would go on to do Friends, Joey and even have his own show on HBO. Hey, who is LeBlanc’s agent? He definitely deserves a raise…
