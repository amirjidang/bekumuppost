Kids Rock and Drag Queen Picture From 2003 Called Out For Hypocrisy

August 2024 · 3 minute read

A Kids Rock and a drag queen photo from 2003 has gone viral, sparking accusations of hypocrisy against the singer. What is it about?

A humorous response on social media has been sparked by a viral picture featuring American singer Kid Rock enjoying a Bud Light beer while partying with a drag queen. 

The image started circulating after the singer shared a video in which he fired bullets at Bud Light beer cans to protest trans activist Dylan Mulvaney’s selection as Anheuser-Busch’s spokesperson. In the picture, Kid Rock can be seen alongside the drag queen, who is wearing a Coors hat. 

Several users took this opportunity to troll the opinionated singer on social media, with one Redditor jokingly stating, “I only crossdrink at parties.”

Kid Rock called out for hypocrisy as viral picture with drag queen Wanda from 2003 resurfaces online

On September 26, 2003, World Red Eye (WRE) captured an iconic image of Kid Rock seated alongside South Beach drag queen Wanda at Prive nightclub in Miami, Florida. 

The photograph was republished as a tribute to Wanda in 2013 after her passing the previous year. Initially the photo was met with amusement by netizens who pointed out the hypocrisy stemming from Rock’s reaction to the Dylan Mulvaney controversy. 

Consequently an array of customized memes and GIFs circulated online inciting further hilarity in regards to his notorious reputation. 

This unique photograph continues to spark conversations amongst social media users around the world regarding Kid Rock’s controversial views on society and further serves to illustrate WRE’s expertise in capturing meaningful moments through their lens.

Travis Tritt also rejects Bud Light following trans activist Dylan Mulvaney’s picture with the beverage

After Dylan Mulvaney, a trans activist, was chosen as the spokesperson for Anheuser-Busch’s Bud Light, country singer Travis Tritt publicly renounced all Anheuser-Busch products from his tour hospitality rider. 

Tritt also criticized the company for being sold to Europeans and becoming “unrecognizable.” He further stated that several other artists are also removing Anheuser-Busch products but are not announcing it publicly due to fear of getting canceled. 

Kid Rock, who recently posted a video protesting Mulvaney’s selection and firing bullets at Bud Light beer cans, has not responded to a viral picture of himself drinking Bud Light while sitting next to drag queen Wanda. 

The image sparked humorous reactions on social media. Kid Rock is not the only person to denounce Anheuser-Busch; several others have also voiced their disapproval of the company’s decision to appoint Mulvaney as its spokesperson. 

Mulvaney had posted a video on social media in which she shared that Bud Light sent her customized beer cans with her face to commemorate her 365 days of being a girl.
