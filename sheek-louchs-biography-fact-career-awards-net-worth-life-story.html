New-York born rap artist Sheek Louch has a net worth of $3 million. Sheek earned his fortune from singing with the rap group, LOX and from his record label known as D-Block records. His inaugural solo album released in 2003 reached number 3 on the Billboard charts and contributed to much of his success. Since then, Louch has managed to release another 5 successful albums.

Born Sean Divine Jacobs in November 9, 1976, Sheek Louch is currently a rapper and a member of LOX which features other popular artists such as Jason Philips (Jadakiss) and Styles P. When aged 12, he was inspired to rhyme by Jadakiss whom he later teamed with to form the LOX. He began showing his entrepreneurial side by forming his record label and looking for fresh talent who wanted to join the industry. He also featured in an episode of the World’s Wildest Police Videos where he showed his notorious life in the past.
