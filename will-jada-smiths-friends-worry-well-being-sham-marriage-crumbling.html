Will And Jada Smiths Friends Allegedly Worry For Their Well-Being As Their Sham Marriage Is

Highlights

  • Will and Jada Smith's marriage is causing concern among their friends, who fear it's a sham ready to explode.
  • They struggle to spend time together as Will focuses on his career and Jada remains committed to never divorcing him.

Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith haven’t been shy to discuss details of their marriage publicly, including their revelation last year that they’ve largely been leading separate lives despite remaining legally married. However, a new report claims that those close to the couple have concern the marriage is going to fall apart soon.

"Will's money keeps Jada living in the style she's accustomed to. But to their friends, this is a sham that's going to explode at some point," a source told National Enquirer.

Jada And Will’s Friends Are Worried Their Marriage Is A “Sham”

As Will focuses on re-establishing his career (following the punch he threw at Chris Rock at the 2022 Academy Awards in defense of Jada), it appears their careers may be getting in the way.

"Will's going to be off doing projects and planning more adventures in the Amazon, where he caught anacondas for his new National Geographic documentary Pole to Pole," the insider continued.

“Will and Jada have little in common and don't even seem to LIKE each other!" they added.

Jada Swears She’ll Never Divorce Will

Earlier this year, Jada confused fans when she said she’s still together with Will, despite the various admissions she made in her memoir released the year prior. Another source claims that while Will and Jada are trying to keep a united front, people are beginning to see through it.

"Will and Jada are putting on a united front, ending their seven-year separation and choosing to live under the same roof once again," a source close to the high-profile couple claimed.

"They are openly declaring their commitment to each other, portraying themselves as a happy couple after the recent messy revelations about their relationship," they went on. Jada and Will haven’t responded to recent speculation over the status of their marriage. But Jada claimed last year during an appearance on the Today show, that she’ll never consider divorcing Will, despite what they may go through.

“I made a promise that there will never be a reason for us to get a divorce,” Jada explained. “We will work through whatever. And I just haven’t been able to break that promise.”

Despite the speculation around their union, Will and Jada remain one of the longest couples in Hollywood. They first met in 1994 when she auditioned for a role on The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, only to marry in 1997. Will and Jada have two children: Jaden (born 1998) and Willow (born 2000).

The couple owns homes in various locations, including Los Angeles and Malibu. They've collaborated on projects like the production company Overbrook Entertainment, which produced films like Ali and The Pursuit of Happyness (in which Will starred alongside their son). Additionally, they've been involved in philanthropy and activism, notably through the Will and Jada Smith Family Foundation.
