Hollywood actress Angelina Jolie is known for being a very hands-on mom.So much so, the millionaire mother of six of her children was seen selling organic dog treats at a dog park with them. She isn't making the kids take a spoiled, easy road. Let's took a look further at what Vivienne and Knox were up to.

Actress Sarah Ramos Spotted The Jolie-Pitt Kids Selling Dog Treats

Oscar-winning actress Angelina Jolie was seen with her 14-year-old twins Vivienne and Knox and 16-year-old Shiloh, and an unidentified woman, at a dog park to sell organic dog treats. Former Parenthood actress Sarah Ramos, 27, shared the photos of the group sitting by a table.

“I’m not sure why God chose me to tell this story but I have to fulfill my calling: A couple months ago I went to the dog park and saw Angelina Jolie selling organic dog treats,” Ramos wrote. She added, “PS the treat sales benefited @hopeforpawsrescue. Donate today to make Angie happy!"

Ramos later told PEOPLE she felt unsure of whether to share the family outing online. "I felt like I needed to protect the privacy of Angie and her family who were just trying to sell some organic dog treats at the dog park,” she said. “I felt really conflicted. I thought, 'This is the story of our times.' Everything was changed after I went to the dog park. I wasn’t expecting anything [to happen when I went there] and I found Angie Jolie.”

“So, also, I know now that Angelina’s kids knew I was taking that photo and I wasn’t getting anything past anybody but I’m still glad I took the photo and I’m sorry that’s their lives,” Ramos continued.

“And they were just selling organic dog treats for some charity. Maybe it was the kids idea to go do something fun in the neighborhood, be a normal mom, a normal fam. Your parents would do it if you asked them.”
