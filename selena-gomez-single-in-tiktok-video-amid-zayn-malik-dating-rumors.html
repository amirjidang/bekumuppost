Selena Gomez Announces She Is Single In A TikTok Video Amid Zayn Malik Dating Rumors

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Selena Gomez has allegedly confirmed her relationship status in a rather unusual way. In a freshly posted TikTok video, the singer can be found screaming "I'm single!" as she watches a football game. She seemed to let the football players know she was open to dating.

"I'm just a little high maintenance, but I'll love you soooo much," she continued. The singer posted the video on her TikTok with the caption "The struggle, man lol." This clip comes after a video she posted where she admitted she was "still looking" for Mr. Right.

The clip has left fans murmuring as it comes out amid rumors of Selena dating singer Zayn Malik. A few months back, the two were spotted together in New York. According to sources, the pair was out for dinner. "Selena and Zayn went out in SoHo in New York City last night at around 10:30 pm," the source claimed. "They walked in holding hands and were kissing. Most restaurant staff and restaurant goers didn't notice them. It seemed like they were comfortable together and it was clear that it was a date."

Selena Was Apparently Linked With Another Singer

The rumors of Selena and Zayn's romance started when a TikTok user shared a screenshot of her conversation with a friend. She claimed that the pair walked into the restaurant where she worked. She also claimed that she was the one who seated her, while they were getting cosy.

However, Zayn isn't the only one linked to the Wolves singer. Before Zayn, Selena was allegedly going out with The Chainsmokers' Drew Taggart. Rumors started to spread after a source claimed the two were a thing. But, Selena was quick to shoot the speculations down. She posted a story on Instagram confirming she "like being alone too much," with the hashtag "#iamsingle."

Zayn Malik Admires Selena And Would Love To Date Her

Although Selena has claimed that she is still single, Zayn seems to be very much interested in taking things further with her. A source revealed Zayn has always admired Selena and thinks she is an "incredible person, inside and out."

The source also claimed that Zayn has been "focused on himself these past few years and feels like he’s ready to date again." "He’s feeling optimistic about the possibility of things growing between him and Selena but isn’t putting any pressure on the situation.
