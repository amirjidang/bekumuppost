DJ D-Wrek Biography

DJ D-Wrek(Deric Battiste)is an American record producer, actor, disk jockey, and television personality who is best known for being the co-host and the DJ of the improv comedy show Wild ‘N Out (an American improv comedy show created and hosted by comedian Nick Cannon) on MTV and MTV2. D-Wrek was named the resident DJ and Master of Ceremonies for the 6th season of MTV’s Wild ‘n Out. In 2004, he performed at Jingle Ball, an annual concert tour series which is organized by iHeartMedia.

DJ D-Wrek Work

D-Wrek began his career earlier performing at the age of 13 in B-Boy Battles. In 1992, he moved to Los Angeles. In 1994, he formed a rap duo called “Makin’ ENZ” with his friend Danny Bell. In 1995, they signed with Caliber Records (titled ‘The number one production label in Florida’).

1997, the duo broke up. In the same year, D-Wrek met Nick Cannon. In the present day, he is represented by Moodswing360 (a boutique touring and marketing agency, with deep roots in NYC dance music and DJ culture) and 1Management.
