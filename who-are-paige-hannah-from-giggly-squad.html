Who Are Paige And Hannah From 'Giggly Squad'?

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Paige and Hannah managed to giggle their way on Summer House into a successful podcast and merchandise line. These best friends met at Betches Media and got along instantaneously. Hannah was already working there as a creative video producer and she eventually started her own podcast, "Berning In Hell." Paige was interviewing for a fashion writer position which she obviously landed. Paige and Hannah first became friends in the work place and their friendship became so much more.

The two were asked to be on season three of the hit reality hit show Summer House and the decision to do it was a no-brainer. They immediately became a trio with Amanda Batula and their friendship seemed unbreakable. A feud broke out on season five between Amanda's fiancé, Kyle Cooke, and Hannah Berner. This led to many explosive fights on and off screen and is the reason Hannah is not returning for season six. Paige Desorbo is closer than ever to Amanda and was just a bridesmaid in her wedding... which Hannah was uninvited to. Let's take a look at where Paige and Hannah stand today and how they got to where they are.

6 Who Is Paige Desorbo?

Paige Desorbo is a a fashion expert who actually started her own fashion website called, "Outfit Deets," and writes "Looks For Less" for Betches Media. She stars on Bravo's Summer House and is known for her bubbly personality and chill attitude. She recently appeared on the spinoff series Winter House where she became cozy with Bravo-lebrity Craig Conover. The two confirmed their relationship and the fans are obsessed with this match made in Bravo heaven.

5 Paige's Boyfriend Craig Conover

Paige had something to say about those commenting on her "fake" relationship. "The number one thing that's really been pissing me off is people are like 'Oh they're just acting and they're doing this as a PR stunt,' " she said. "If that was true, I would straight Ben and Jen this s--- and be posting it all over and I'd have the best captions to everything." She added, "The only reason I haven't posted him is I haven't taken a grid-worthy pic. And this is my real-life boyfriend, you're not posting your boyfriend on your Instagram until he says 'I love you.'"

Craig better drop the L-word soon so we can see pictures of these two all over our 'gram! Paige is still filming for Summer House and balancing her work relationship with Hannah.

4 Who Is Hannah Berner?

Hannah Berner is a comedian, entrepreneur, podcaster and starred on three seasons of Summer House. Currently, Hannah is engaged to comedian, Des Bishop, who is 45 years old. Hannah is a co-host on Bravo’s “Chat Room,” “Berning in Hell,” as well as "Giggly Squad."

Hannah was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, and played tennis competitively for nearly fifteen years. Hannah was beloved on Summer House for her first two seasons until she turned into the villain the last time around. She was known for being witty, sarcastic, and relatable. Unfortunately, that all took a turn last summer and she is now leaving the show for good.

3 Hannah Leaves 'Summer House'

Hannah broke the news on her Instagram writing, “I look forward to an exciting summer, but with mixed emotions, I’m announcing I won’t be spending it living in the summer house. These last three summers have truly been a whirlwind and the show has, without a doubt, changed my life and I will forever be thankful for the platform it has provided me. Making people laugh is what brings me joy and I am so excited to announce that I will be spending this summer touring my standup and telling fart jokes at comedy clubs and theaters near you.”

Hannah is busy wedding planning, and focusing on her comedy career and her new life post-reality show. She is still giggling away with Paige on their podcast, but their relationship has had its fair share of ups and downs.

2 Giggly Squad Podcast

"Welcome to 'Giggly Squad'. Each week, Hannah Berner and Paige DeSorbo make fun of everything, but most importantly themselves. They discuss pop culture, fashion trends, television, horoscopes, mental health, dating, and expose their personal lives. Also, they can't be managed."

"Giggly Squad" all started thanks to cast member Kyle Cooke who yelled at the ladies at dinner for giggling while he was talking. This one scene birthed an entire business all thanks to somebody who doesn't even speak to one of the leading ladies anymore. Their podcast has been going on since October 2020 and has over 50 episodes. Thereare also "Giggly Squad" hoodies, sweatpants, shirts, hats and accessories!

1 Where Do Paige and Hannah Stand?

Recently, Paige and Hannah went on the "Call Her Daddy" podcast and had one heck of a therapy session. Alex Cooper did not hold back when it came to asking hard questions about their relationship and everything that went down during Summer House. Paige revealed she was relieved that her best friend decided to not return to the show because there was so much animosity when they were all in a room together. Hannah admitted that she felt like she lost everything after the show aired and she was not going to lose Paige too.

We hope that these two continue pushing their relationship forward and keep giggling their way to total stardom!
