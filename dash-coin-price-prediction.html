What Is Dash (DASH)?

Dash is a cryptocurrency that works likewise to Bitcoin. Dash’s blockchain forked off from Bitcoin’s. In 2014 it was created. Dash initially used the name Darkcoin, hinting at anonymity and privacy for its transactions.

Over the times, Dash redirected its center from providing anonymous transactions to pace and effectiveness. Hence, the name Dash is formed.

According to its website, Dash is popularly used internationally as a more practical option to cash or credit cards, and this is because of its economical fees and quick transactions.

Dash has been consistently on the top 100 list for years. It has an all-time high (ATH) price of $1,642.22 per coin, according to CoinMarketCap.
