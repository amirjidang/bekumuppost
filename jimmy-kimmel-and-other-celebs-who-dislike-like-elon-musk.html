Jimmy Kimmel And 12 Other Celebs Who've Got No Love For Elon Musk

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Near the end of 2022, Jimmy Kimmel took shots at Elon Musk in one of his monologues. "You got no love for Elon, Bro... No, I don't." Those were the comedian's exact words. But Kimmel is not the only celebrity who is not a fan of the Twitter-addicted billionaire. Numerous celebrities, including a few politicians, have butted heads with Musk.Related: 8 Celebrities Who Have Said They're Leaving Twitter Due To Elon Musk's TakeoverFrom petty disagreements to dissatisfied Tesla customers, Musk has both taken shots from and swings at many famous people, even at some of his fellow billionaires. These are the musicians, actors, and politicians, who also "got no love," for Elon Musk.

13 Bernie Sanders

Wait, the most popular socialist politician in the U.S. is not a fan of the world's former richest man? Shocking! Bernie is notorious for his disdain for the upper class, "Billionaires Should Not Exist," was one of his 2020 presidential campaign talking points. But things got personal when Bernie tweeted about taxing billionaires. Musk tweeted back "I keep forgetting you're still alive." Sure Elon, you "forgot" that one of the most popular elected officials in America was still around.

12 Sarah Palin

While Bernie hit Musk from the Left, he received blows from the right as well. Former governor and failed vice presidential candidate Sarah Palin took a jab at Elon's car company, calling Tesla a "loser." She went on to attack the concept of electric cars, saying that they turn into "bricks" when the batteries die out.

11 Johnny Depp (Allegedly)

During the highly public defamation case between Johnny Depp and Amber Heard, a series of texts written by Depp attacking Musk were entered into evidence. After Depp and Heard broke up, she started a relationship with Musk, and allegedly she was already sleeping with him when they were still married. Depp routinely insulted Musk in his texts, calling the billionaire "Elon Mollusk" and other names. However, when the verdict was announced Musk voiced support for both Depp and Heard, saying they're both "fine people" when they're at their best.

10 Elton John

Numerous celebrities quit Twitter after Elon Musk took over because they were worried that Musk's removal of content moderation protocols would lead to spikes in hateful tweets and misinformation. Among them was rock legend Elton John. "I’ve decided to no longer use Twitter, given their recent change in policy which will allow misinformation to flourish unchecked."

9 Gigi Hadid

Hadid took a direct jab at Musk's management style when she announced she was leaving Twitter. "For a long time, but especially with its new leadership, it’s becoming more and more of a cesspool of hate & bigotry, and it's not a place I want to be a part of."

8 Whoopi Goldberg

Goldberg is yet another star to leave Twitter because of Musk's takeover. In an episode of The View, the Oscar winner made it clear why she thought Musk's takeover has been a mess, "I just feel like it’s so messy, and I’m tired of now having had certain kinds of attitudes blocked, and now they’re back on."

7 Jameela Jamil

Jamil had already endured attacks and trolling via Twitter long before Musk took over, but his policy changes were the final straw for the actress. "One good thing about Elon buying Twitter is that I will FINALLY leave and stop being a complete menace to society on here." According to Rolling Stone, other stars who quit the app thanks to Musk are Jim Carrey, Meek Mill, Shonda Rimes, Alex Winter, Tea Leoni, Jack White, and Trent Reznor. There are probably several more they missed.

6 George Clooney

Clooney was an early Tesla owner, and his final review of the product was not good. According to Clooney, his car broke down constantly, forcing him off the road on multiple occasions. Musk's retort was petty and an example of terrible customer service, "In other news, George Clooney reports that his iPhone 1 had a bug back in '07." Hey Elon, it's your job to make sure your company makes a quality product. Whatever happened to "the customer is always right"?

5 Jeremy Clarkson

If a car expert doesn't like the car you make, you have a problem. The Top Gear host did not give Tesla rave reviews. In a 2008 segment, they showed a Tesla with failing brakes and a depleting electrical charge, which the company then alleged was staged. Tesla sued Clarkson for libel in 2011 but lost. Since then, Clarkson has made his feelings about Musk very clear. "He's the only one who ever behaved in such a petulant way — most industry bosses are a lot more grown-up."

4 Jeff Bezos

Musk has Tweeted fat jokes about Bill Gates and took jabs at the way Mark Zuckerberg runs Facebook (now called Meta), but it would seem the Amazon founder gets the most hate. The two had a major falling out over the contract for a former NASA launching pad, a part of the ongoing "space race" of billionaires. Bezo's even hired a former SpaceX employee, allegedly to spite Musk.

3 Azealia Banks

Okay, Banks is famous for feuding with quite a few celebrities. That said, she still had some harsh words for Elon. Allegedly, a visit to the Musk household left Banks feeling creeped out, and she claims to have witnessed Musk have a full meltdown over the phone. She also is on record calling Musk a "beta" and mocking his alleged hair plugs.

Related: A Timeline Of Azealia Banks’ Biggest Beefs Over The Years

2 Megan McCain

Elon Musk has a bizarre obsession with the idea that underpopulation is a global crisis, despite being disproven by experts. The former host of The View called Musk's obsession "creepy" and "disingenuous" in an op-ed for The Daily Mail.

1 Jimmy Kimmel

As mentioned before, Kimmel made it clear that he has "no love for Elon bro," in one of his show monologues. Kimmel was not happy that Musk mocked both transpeople and the Covid pandemic with a painfully unfunny jab at Dr. Anthony Fauci. Musk Tweeted, "My pronouns are prosecute/fauci." To which Kimmel pointed out that Fauci had been a dedicated public servant through several major American health crises, not just Covid. He even tweeted back at Elon, "your pronouns are ass and hole."
