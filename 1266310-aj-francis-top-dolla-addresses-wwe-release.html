AJ Francis (Top Dolla) Releases Song Thank You // 89 Days To Address His WWE Release

August 2024 · 2 minute read

says he’s got 89 days before he makes another splash in the professional wrestling industry.

AJ Francis, aka Top Dolla of Hit Row, was released by WWE on Thursday, November 18. Francis, Swerve Scott and Ashante Adonis were three of the eight names cut by WWE, with budget cuts being cited as the reason for the move.

On Friday, he posted a new track called “Thank You // 89 Days” to comment on his release and address his future in the wrestling industry.

Thank you, thank you for all the love / Thank you, thank you, thank you, you’re far too kind…

Hold your applause, this is your song, not mines / What a time to be alive, not everybody lives but everybody dies

Don’t let me catch you with no tears inside ya eyes / My feeling inside is the fear will subside when you know you living right

They gave me 90 days pay and Ima take that / And I’m still taking over the game, no take backs

So since we’re gonna state facts, I’m going way, way, way, way back like an 8-track

From the day that I signed the company line was just grind, in due time you’ll get your shine

But now I gotta shine somewhere else and that’s their problem / Making money ain’t nothin’ but math and Ima solve it

Play Russian roulette with a fully loaded revolver and you’d still have a better shot than me not evolving

In 90 days, I’m everywhere / Tell ya favorite promotion that you want me there

I’m trynna take my talents all across the land and over to Japan just because I know I can

And if you’re AEW, ROH, IMPACT, New Japan, NWA, PWG, MLW, DDT, All Japan, [GCW and more] and you see me as somebody you trynna get on the dotted line / 89 days from now, I can sign

Francis went on to address his celebrity status prior to signing with WWE, as he made many appearances on FOX Sports due to his days as a former NFL player.
