10 Danny Pudi: $3 Million

Danny Pudi's character Abed Nadir was one of fan favorites and one of the actor's first roles. He is worth $3 million. All that money didn't just come from Community, though. He also appeared in several television advertisements and starred in the comedy horror film Knights of Badassdom (2013).

9 Yvette Nicole Brown: $4 Milion

The woman behind Community's Shirley Bennet is Yvette Nicole Brown, a writer and a comedian who also worked on TV shows, such as Mom, That '70s Show, and The Office. She is worth $4 million.

She left the show after its fifth season because she chose to go care for her father.

8 Gillian Jacobs: $5 Million

The stunning Gillian who played Britta is worth $5 million. She is also known for her roles of Mickey Dobbs on Netflix's Love and Mimi-Rose Howard on Girls. Gillian attended the Juilliard School where she received her BA in 2004.

In 2020, she starred in I Used To Go Here, a comedy-drama about a woman who is invited to speak at her alma mater and finds herself getting involved in other students' lives.

Jim Rash is mostly known for his portrayal of Dean Craig Pelton, but he is also a screenwriter. He wrote the screenplay for The Descendants (2011), starring George Clooney and Shailene Woodley and even received an Academy Award. He also co-directed and co-wrote The Way, Way Back (20013), touching coming-of-age drama.

There is more to the man who plays the wacky dean than meets the eye. He is currently worth $8 million.

6 Alison Brie: $10 Million

Alison Brie is most famous for her roles as Annie on Community, Diane on BoJack Horseman, and Trudy on Mad Men. In 2017, she landed a starring role on GLOW. She's currently worth $10 million.
