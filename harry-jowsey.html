Netflix Reality Series – Too Hot To Handle

The 22-years-old Harry Jowsey rose to fame after appearing in the Netflix series “Too Hot to Handle”. It came out on April 17, 2020, on the platform. The game show was produced by Fremantle production company Talkback.

Lana worked as a host of the web series. During the trailer of the show, Jowsey was seen kissing and getting cozy with a fellow contestant named Francesca Farago (Canadian Instagram Influencer and Model).

The series consisted of eight episodes which all came out on April 17, 2020. One of the main conditions of the show was that if contestants engage in sexual contact then the prize money would decrease.

At the finale, Harry Jowsey along with David Birtwistle, Lydia Clyma, Bryce Hirschberg, Kelechi Kelz Dyke, Chloe Veitch, Nicole O’Brien, Rhonda Paul, Sharron Townsend, and Francesca Farago announced as winners of the web series.

The winners split $75,000 US Dollars among each other. Some of the other contestants of the show were Haley Cureton, Matthew Smith, Kori Sampson, and Madison Wyborny. The show was filmed at Casa Tau in Punta Mita, Mexico.
