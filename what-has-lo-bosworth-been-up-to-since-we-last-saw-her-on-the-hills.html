What Has Lo Bosworth Been Up To Since We Last Saw Her On The Hills?

August 2024 Â· 5 minute read

Lo Bosworth was first introduced to the world on Laguna Beach where she was Lauren Conrad's best friend and hilarious sidekick. She then continued to make appearances on The Hills years later. So, what exactly has Bosworth been up to now that she's no longer making appearances on reality television? Fans may be curious to know, especially with the Back to the Beach rewatch podcast in full swing.

Related: Here's How MTV's Laguna Beach Really Came About

Bosworth has actually been quite busy and does in fact manage to keep in touch with her best friend, Conrad, even though they now live on opposite coasts. Bosworth has lived in New York City for the past ten years and has a very different life now than she did ten years ago. She has been through a lot, from breakups to injuries, but spends her time now focusing on wellness. Let's find out what Lo Bosworth is up to these days!

8 Lo Bosworth Suffered A Traumatic Brain Injury

In an Instagram post in 2021, Bosworth opened up about a traumatic brain injury she suffered two years prior. "I was at a restaurant in lower Manhattan (which shall remain nameless because accidents happen) and while sitting in a banquet had one of the swinging kitchen doors fall off it's hinges and on to my head. I was in the hospital and suffered a moderate to severe concussion for months. I took weeks off work and the road to recovery was long. I remember visiting a friend a few weeks later and feeling totally lost on 3rd avenue and 21st street, not knowing which direction to walk in."

7 Lo Bosworth Keeps In Touch With Lauren Conrad

In a TikTok video posted in the Summer of 2022, Bosworth answered a fan question about whether or not she still keeps in touch with her pal, Lauren Conrad. Bosworth stated that "Yep! I've lived on the East Coast for more than 10 years so we don't chat super frequently but definitely still friends." Fans of Laguna Beach were probably very delighted to hear that the two are still friends and manage to keep in touch every so often in between their busy lives and hectic schedules, especially since Conrad is a mother of two young boys.

6 Lo Bosworth Lives in NYC

Lo Bosworth has lived in New York City for over 10 years now. She moved there in 2012 and ended up loving it so much that she ended up staying. She mentioned on the Unzipped podcast that she feels a lot more comfortable living life on her own terms and not having to share her life on television in the world of reality TV. She also stated that she always wanted to go to college and live a normal life, and escaping to New York was her way of doing just that.

5 Lo Bosworth Is Not Married

Bosworth has had several relationships with men over the years but has not married. While on The Hills, she was dating a guy named Scott Hochstadt, but they ended their relationship about five months after the show concluded its run. In May 2019, it was reported that Bosworth was dating Shark Tank contestant, Jimmy DeCicco, but as of this writing, they are no longer together, as all of their social media posts featuring the two of them have been deleted. Bosworth is believed to be single currently, but as she's pretty private about her dating life, no one knows for sure.

4 Lo Bosworth Worked On A Book

Bosworth has a book coming out in 2023 called Love Yourself Well and it is "an empowering wellness guide to supporting your gut, brain and vagina." According to the product description, Bosworth began suffering from yeast infections out of nowhere as well as fatigue and brain fog. From there, she began her journey to health and wellness and shares that journey in this book.

3 Lo Bosworth Attended Culinary School

In a TikTok video, Bosworth stated that she first moved to NYC to attend culinary school because she wanted to have her own cooking show. She said that she loves to cook, and while at culinary school, she became very interested in nutrition, which lead her on her path to creating her own business based on wellness.

2 Lo Bosworth Created Love Wellness

Bosworth is the founder and CEO of a company called Love Wellness that sells multivitamins, supplements, and personal care products for women. They offer a subscription service where customers can regularly get their products shipped to them so that they never run out.

1 Lo Bosworth Has A YouTube Channel

Bosworth has had a YouTube channel for several years now where she posts videos about health, wellness, and beauty. She has over 100 thousand subscribers and has been a member of YouTube since 2013 when she began sharing her knowledge in the culinary world. She took an extended break from her channel for a few years but recently returned to help promote her Love Wellness company.
