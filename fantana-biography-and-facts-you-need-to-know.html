Fantana Biography And Facts You Need To Know

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Francine Koffie, well known as Fantana, is a Ghanaian singer formerly signed unto the Rufftown records label. Her mother is a politician who was recently elected as Member of Parliament for Jomoro constituency.

Biography

Francine Koffie popularly known as Fantana was born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia in the United States of America where she spent most of her life. She was born on July 3, 1997.
She is from  Nzema land in the Western Region of Ghana. She schooled in the United States throughout. Business and Fashion are the courses she read in School.

Even though Fantana studied business and fashion in school, she couldn’t hold back her passion for music. The famous musician decided to return to her home country, Ghana, to launch her career. And that is how she managed to become one of Ghana’s fastest rising musicians.
Fantana revealed that growing up her friends used to call her Fantana, a name Bullet her former boss suggested she uses as a stage name. She is no longer signed under Ruff town record label owing to a misunderstanding between herself and the CEO of Rufftown records.
